Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,

Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries and creamy brie. It will become your favourite holiday startser

Ingredients
For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
For the topping:
  • 43 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
Method
  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghter all the dough Ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually kneas the dough, tought I sometimes knead it a few times.
